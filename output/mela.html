Mela

Mela is a dark comedy about Time. Mela is a badly sewn patch in front of which we can't but cover our eyes. Three women, three generations facing each other. Each one represents the collapse of the principles built and reaffirmed by the previous generation. Principles that, more than 25 years later, are still call into question in building a contemporary society.

In loving memory of Silvia Giuntoli

Words by Dacia Maraini.
With Silvia Giuntoli, Debora Pradarelli and Briana Zaki.

Pictures by Tamara Casula.

Directed by Irene Ros.
